## What's the Difference?

### Dataset Search (Where you are now)
- **Purpose**: Find/search for specific datasets (collections, projects, etc.)
- **Use case**: Research specific data sources
- **Not for**: Downloading occurrence records

### Occurrence Download (Where you need to go)
- **Purpose**: Download occurrence records (species observations)
- **Use case**: Get all species observations for a country/region
- **Filters**: Country, date range, coordinates, taxa, etc.
- **Output**: CSV/TSV file with occurrence records

---

## Quick Steps After Reaching Occurrence Download Page:

1. **Set Country filter**: Italy (IT) or Greece (GR)
2. **Add "Has coordinate: Yes"** (required for geospatial analysis)
3. **Optional filters**: Date range, taxa, etc.
4. **Click "Request download"**
5. **Wait for email notification**
6. **Download when ready**
