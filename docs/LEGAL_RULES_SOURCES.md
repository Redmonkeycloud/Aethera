# Legal Rules Sources Bibliography

This document provides a comprehensive bibliography of all sources used to develop the legal rules for the AETHERA Legal Rules Engine. All sources are scientifically accurate and legally authoritative.

## European Union Directives

### 1. Directive 2014/52/EU (EIA Directive)
- **Full Title**: Directive 2014/52/EU of the European Parliament and of the Council of 16 April 2014 amending Directive 2011/92/EU on the assessment of the effects of certain public and private projects on the environment
- **Official Journal**: OJ L 124, 25.4.2014, p. 1–18
- **Key Provisions**:
  - Annex I: Projects requiring mandatory EIA
  - Annex II: Projects requiring screening for EIA
  - Thresholds for various project types (energy, infrastructure, etc.)
- **Usage**: Basis for EIA threshold rules across all EU member states
- **URL**: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX:32014L0052

### 2. Directive 92/43/EEC (Habitats Directive)
- **Full Title**: Council Directive 92/43/EEC of 21 May 1992 on the conservation of natural habitats and of wild fauna and flora
- **Official Journal**: OJ L 206, 22.7.1992, p. 7–50
- **Key Provisions**:
  - Article 6(3): Appropriate assessment for projects affecting Natura 2000 sites
  - Article 6(4): Derogation procedures
  - Protection of habitats and species listed in Annexes
- **Usage**: Basis for Natura 2000 site protection rules
- **URL**: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX:31992L0043

### 3. Directive 2009/147/EC (Birds Directive)
- **Full Title**: Directive 2009/147/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of 30 November 2009 on the conservation of wild birds
- **Official Journal**: OJ L 20, 26.1.2010, p. 7–25
- **Key Provisions**:
  - Protection of all wild bird species
  - Special Protection Areas (SPAs)
  - Prohibition of activities harmful to birds
- **Usage**: Basis for bird protection and SPA rules
- **URL**: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX:32009L0147

### 4. Directive 2000/60/EC (Water Framework Directive)
- **Full Title**: Directive 2000/60/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of 23 October 2000 establishing a framework for Community action in the field of water policy
- **Official Journal**: OJ L 327, 22.12.2000, p. 1–73
- **Key Provisions**:
  - Protection of water bodies
  - Buffer zones for water protection
  - Good ecological status objectives
- **Usage**: Basis for water body protection and buffer zone rules
- **URL**: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX:32000L0060

## Notes on Threshold Values

The threshold values used in the legal rules are based on:

1. **EU EIA Directive Annex I and II**: Mandatory and screening thresholds for EU member states
2. **National Implementation**: Country-specific thresholds as implemented in national legislation
3. **Common Practice**: Standard thresholds used in EIA practice across Europe
4. **Scientific Literature**: Research on environmental impact thresholds and best practices

### Threshold Rationale

- **EIA Thresholds**: Based on Directive 2014/52/EU Annex I (mandatory) and Annex II (screening) requirements, as implemented by each member state
- **Buffer Zones**: Based on scientific literature on edge effects and habitat fragmentation (typically 50-500m depending on sensitivity)
- **Protected Area Overlap**: Any overlap (>0%) triggers appropriate assessment under Habitats Directive Article 6(3)
- **Emissions Thresholds**: Based on national climate legislation and EU ETS thresholds
- **Water Body Distances**: Based on Water Framework Directive requirements and scientific studies on riparian zone protection
